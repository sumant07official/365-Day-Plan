365 DAY DISCIPLINE SYSTEM

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

🔒 Short version: This app stores all your data locally on your device only. We collect nothing. We share nothing. There are no servers.

1. Data We Collect

The 365 Day Discipline System does not collect any personal data. All habit tracking data, streaks, challenge progress, and settings are stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage on your own device.

We do not have access to your data. Your data never leaves your device.

2. Local Storage

The app uses your browser's local storage to save your habit playlists, daily check-in records, challenge progress, and app preferences. This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

3. No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use any analytics tools, tracking pixels, or third-party scripts. The only external resource loaded is Google Fonts for display purposes.

4. No Account Required

This app requires no account, no sign-up, and no email address. There is no login system.
